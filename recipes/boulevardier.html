Boulevardier

Description

Employing whiskey, sweet vermouth and Campari, the Boulevardier is simply a variation on the classic Negroni that is adored for its deft balance of bitter, boozy and sweet. The difference in flavor, however, is anything but simple. While the gin-based Negroni is crisp and bracing, the whiskey-based Boulevardier is rich and warming. Itâ€™s the exact drink you want to reach for on a chilly fall or winter evening.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 oz bourbon
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • Orange twist garnish

Steps

  1. Add bourbon, Campari, and sweet vermouth into a mixing glass and stir until chilled.
  2. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with an orange twist.
